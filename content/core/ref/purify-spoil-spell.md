## Description

### Purify

You purify spoiled food or drink equal to one of the following:

- **Water**: 1 gallon
- **Ration**: 1 ration (iron or standard)
- **Regular Food**: Amount to feed 10 people.

### Spoil

As per purify, but you make it spoiled instead of purified.
